# Drivo
Drivo is a mobile app that makes car rental fast, simple, and transparent. Users get personalized car suggestions, clear pricing, and a fully digital booking with ID upload, contract summary, and online payment.

Car owners can list their vehicles and choose whether to provide a driver or let renters drive themselves. Drivo also partners with rental offices, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both owners and users.
